ABOUT US



"Welcome to GrocieMart, your neighborhood supermarket dedicated to providing you with the freshest and highest-quality groceries. With multiple branches across Sri Lanka, including Wattala, Kolonnawa, Dehiwala, Kollupitiya, and Grandpass, we are committed to serving communities and offering a convenient shopping experience.


Our journey began with a vision to redefine the grocery shopping experience by offering a wide range of products sourced directly from local farmers and trusted suppliers. Over the years, we have grown from a single store to a network of branches, each catering to the unique needs and preferences of our diverse customer base.


At GrocieMart, sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We prioritize eco-friendly practices and work closely with suppliers who share our commitment to environmental responsibility. Our aim is to promote local agriculture, reduce food waste, and contribute positively to the communities we serve.


As we continue to grow, our focus remains on delivering exceptional service, quality products, and a seamless shopping experience for our valued customers. Thank you for choosing GrocieMart as your trusted grocery partner."
